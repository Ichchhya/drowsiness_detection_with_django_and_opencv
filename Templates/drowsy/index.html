INTRODUCTION

Drowsiness detection is a system that detects whether or not the person is feeling drowsy. In the current context, people are living a busy life where time management is one of the major problem. More and more professions require long term concentration. But they are unable to manage their time for professional life and personal life. This results in lack of time for food,rest and also entertainment. So, a system that can alert a person who s/he is feeling drowsy is necessary for people in various fields to keep a close eye on their works, so that they can react to any sudden events immediately; be it a student who is unable to concentrate in his study, or a worker who may not alert during his working hours or a driver who may be very tired and drowsy while driving.In case of drivers,fatigue often becomes direct cause of accidents. Therefore, it has become a necessity to develop a systems that detects the drowsiness and alerts them. This system is based on the real time facial images analysis for warning the person of drowsiness and will be a video based system that measures PERCLOS(Percentage of Eye Closure) , a scientifically supported measure of drowsiness associated with slow eye closure.
